[color=yellow]Rogue At The Ren Faire[/color]

Incomplete entry for 7DRL 2026. You can still have some fun with it though!

[b]Movement[/b]
WASD / Arrow keys — cardinal directions
Numpad 7/9/1/3 — diagonal movement (but not on web build?)
Space / Numpad 5 — wait a turn
Left click — move toward clicked tile (click yourself to wait)

[b]Pickpocketing[/b]
Bump into a Mark to attempt a pickpocket. Approaching from behind is safer than from the front.

[b]Guards[/b]
Guards patrol the faire grounds. If a guard catches you stealing, they will chase you. Talk your way out or run!

[b]Merchants and Fences[/b]
Merchants will sell you food and other items, but they won't buy your stolen goods.
Fences will purchase your stolen goods. Try haggling for a better price!

[b]Escaping[/b]
Head to any gate and walk through to end your run. Your score is calculated from FUN earned and money taken.

[b]Score[/b] = FUN points + Money bonus
Earn FUN by pickpocketing, surviving guard chases, and winning coin flip challenges.
